Ivy Montgomery

Musician & Singer

Ivy Montgomery is a Dublin-based Irish vocalist known for her hauntingly melancholic acoustic sound. Behind the scenes, every song is written, composed, and produced by Oğuzhan Bilgin, crafting a deeply personal sonic world through her voice. Rooted in raw emotion and minimalist textures, the music blends poetic storytelling with stripped-down arrangements — a quiet echo of heartbreak, memory, and longing.
