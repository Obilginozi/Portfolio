About Ivy

Musical Journey

Ivy Montgomery is an independent musician and artist known for her ethereal vocals and introspective songwriting. With influences ranging from indie folk to alternative rock, Ivy creates music that speaks to the soul.

Her journey began in the vibrant music scene of London, where she honed her craft in intimate venues and recording studios. Today, she continues to push boundaries and explore new sonic territories.

Ivy's music has been featured on various platforms and has garnered a dedicated following of music lovers who appreciate authentic storytelling and emotional depth.
